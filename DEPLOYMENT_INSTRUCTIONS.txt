NETLIFY DEPLOYMENT

1. Download Love357_AI_V1_Beta_Release.zip.
2. Extract the ZIP on your phone or computer.
3. Open Netlify and choose your existing site.
4. Go to Deploys.
5. Drag the Love357_AI_V1_Beta folder into the deployment area.
   On mobile, you may need to upload the extracted folder files through GitHub instead.
6. Wait for the deploy to finish and open the Netlify URL.
7. Test in a private/incognito window so the setup wizard starts with clean data.

For a brand-new Netlify Drop, drag the Love357_AI_V1_Beta folder to app.netlify.com/drop. Netlify will create a new URL.

Before inviting customers, tell them this is a Founders Circle Beta and not to enter sensitive financial, medical, or confidential information.
